### Decision 001

**The app captures evidence, AI creates knowledge.**

App stores:

- Zone
- Health Area
- Severity
- Photos
- Voice

AI generates:

- Finding title
- Description
- Recommendation
- Checklist mapping

**Reason:**  
Reduce inspector workload while keeping structured data.

# Decision 002 — Property Health is Deterministic

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-07-04

## Decision

Property Health is never generated by AI.

It is calculated deterministically in n8n before data is written to Airtable.

Rules:

- No findings → Green
- At least one Monitor finding → Yellow
- At least one Immediate Action finding → Red

Immediate Action always overrides Monitor.

## Rationale

Property Health is a business rule, not an AI opinion.



# Decision 003 — One Source of Truth

**Status:** Accepted

The app is **not** the database.

The app submits an inspection package to n8n.

n8n enriches it and writes all final records into Airtable.

The PDF and Owner Portal are generated from Airtable.

This guarantees that every interface presents the same underlying data.
